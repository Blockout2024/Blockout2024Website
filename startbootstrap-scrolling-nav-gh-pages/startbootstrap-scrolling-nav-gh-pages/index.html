About Blockout2024

Blockout2024 is a platform dedicated to monitoring and tracking celebrities' social media followings in real-time, focusing specifically on their engagement with the cause of Palestine. In response to the global movement advocating for justice and awareness regarding the Palestinian struggle, Blockout2024 aims to:

  • Provide real-time updates on the following counts of celebrities who have voiced support for Palestine
  • Offer insightful analysis and visualizations of fluctuations in celebrity followings over time
  • Empower users to stay informed about the stance of influential figures on the Palestinian cause
  • Promote transparency and accountability by monitoring celebrities' actions and statements related to Palestine
  • Guide people to invest their money in the right organizations to help the palestinians
